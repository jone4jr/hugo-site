This section is the home for the 5AM Shift weekly stock watchlist.

The format is long-only and built for disciplined review: ranked candidates, buy zones, invalidation levels, target areas, risk/reward, and quick notes on why each company made the list. It does not publish short recommendations.

The watchlist starts with price, volume, trend, and sector strength. The deeper question is management quality: whether the company has the leadership, operating discipline, and execution capacity to keep translating growth into durable results.
