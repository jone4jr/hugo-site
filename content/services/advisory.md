## Specialized Manufacturing Advisory

The automotive supply chain is complex, relationship-driven, and demanding. Success requires not just technical knowledge but deep understanding of customer requirements, capacity planning, and the intricate dance between Tier 1, 2, and 3 suppliers.

### Advisory Services

**Supplier Development & Navigation**
- Help Tier 2/3 suppliers understand and meet customer-specific requirements
- Navigate OEM and Tier 1 quality system demands
- Develop supplier scorecards and performance metrics
- Build long-term customer relationships

**RFQ Response & Capacity Analysis**
- Realistic capacity assessment and costing
- Make-vs-buy analysis
- Production line optimization for new programs
- Risk assessment and mitigation planning

**Customer Audit Preparation**
- Comprehensive facility readiness assessment
- Documentation review and gap closure
- Mock audits and team preparation
- Corrective action planning

**Statistical Process Control Implementation**
- SPC system design and deployment
- Control plan development
- Operator training and engagement
- Data analysis and continuous improvement

### Why Manufacturing Advisory Matters

Manufacturing is still fundamentally about relationships and physical-world problem-solving. While AI can analyze data, it cannot:

- Build trust with quality managers during facility tours
- Read the subtle signs of process instability on a production line
- Negotiate realistic delivery schedules based on actual capacity
- Mentor your team through their first customer audit

### Value Proposition

- Deep automotive supply chain expertise
- Real-world experience with customer audits and certifications
- Practical, implementable solutions
- Ongoing support through implementation
- Network of industry contacts and resources
