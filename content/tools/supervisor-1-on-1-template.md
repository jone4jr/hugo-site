Use this template when you want a one-on-one to do more than trade status updates.

The best leadership check-ins do four things: they clarify what matters most, surface friction early, coach on one real growth area, and make the next commitment visible enough that neither person has to rely on memory. That is what this template is built to support.

If your one-on-ones tend to drift, feel overly reactive, or end without a clear next step, start here.
