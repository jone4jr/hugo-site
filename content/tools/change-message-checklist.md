Change gets harder when leaders assume the message was obvious the first time.

People need clearer signal than most leaders think: what is changing, what is staying the same, why this matters, what success looks like, and who owns the next move. This checklist is built to make that communication easier to repeat and easier to remember.

Use it before launching a new expectation, new process, staffing shift, meeting rhythm, or operating change.
