## What this profile helps you see

This assessment is designed for self-reflection, not diagnosis. It helps you understand your default leadership style in day-to-day work:

- how you communicate when the pressure rises
- where you naturally create momentum
- where your style can create friction with others
- what kind of growth move will make you more effective next

## What you get after finishing

- your likely primary and secondary DISC-style tendencies
- a short description of how your style shows up in leadership
- strengths your team probably experiences from you
- watch-outs to manage before they become blind spots

The best use of this profile is to compare it with how your team actually experiences you.
