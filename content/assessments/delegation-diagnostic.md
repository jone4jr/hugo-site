## Why this one matters

A lot of strong leaders struggle with delegation because they were rewarded for being dependable individual performers first.

This diagnostic looks at four areas that usually determine whether delegation works:

- clarity
- trust
- coaching
- follow-through

If one of those areas is weak, delegation starts to feel risky and leaders pull work back toward themselves.
