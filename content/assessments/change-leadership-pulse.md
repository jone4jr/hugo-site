## Why leaders use this pulse check

Teams do not resist every change. They resist unclear change, inconsistent change, and unsupported change.

This pulse is built to help leaders reflect on how they lead through transitions:

- how clearly they explain what is changing
- whether they create enough rhythm and reinforcement
- how much ownership the team feels
- whether they make room for people concerns without losing pace
