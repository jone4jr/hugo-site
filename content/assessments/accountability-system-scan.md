## Why this one matters

Accountability does not improve because leaders say the word more often. It improves when the system around expectations, ownership, visibility, and consequences becomes clear enough that people know what will happen next.

Use this scan when commitments are missed, standards drift, or leaders feel stuck between micromanaging and hoping.
