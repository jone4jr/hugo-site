## Portfolio Performance Tracking

Real-time tracking of active positions from weekly stock picks. Full transparency on entries, exits, wins, losses, and overall performance metrics.

### Current Holdings

Active positions are updated as picks are executed and managed according to technical signals and risk management rules.

### Performance Metrics

- **Total Return** - Overall portfolio performance vs benchmarks
- **Win Rate** - Percentage of profitable trades
- **Average R/R** - Risk/reward ratios achieved
- **Max Drawdown** - Largest peak-to-trough decline

### Position Management

All positions follow strict risk management:
- **Maximum position size:** 5% per stock
- **Stop losses:** Always defined before entry
- **Target scaling:** Partial exits at T1, T2, T3
- **Portfolio heat:** Maximum 20% total risk exposure
