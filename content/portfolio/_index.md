## Portfolio Philosophy

This portfolio follows a concentrated value investing approach:

- **10-15 positions** maximum
- **15-20% position sizing** for high conviction ideas  
- **3-5 year holding periods** typical
- **Value + Quality focus** - no deep value traps

## Current Holdings

Portfolio positions are updated monthly. Each holding represents a business we believe is trading significantly below intrinsic value with a clear catalyst path.

## Performance Tracking

Returns are calculated net of all fees and compared against the S&P 500 total return benchmark. Full transparency on wins, losses, and lessons learned.

## Risk Management

- Maximum 25% in any single position
- Maximum 40% in any single sector
- Minimum 15% cash position maintained
- Stop losses on turnaround/special situation plays only
