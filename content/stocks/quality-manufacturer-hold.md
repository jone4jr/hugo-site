## Investment Thesis

Quality Manufacturing Corp is a well-run business with strong fundamentals, but current valuation offers limited margin of safety. While we appreciate the quality, we're waiting for a better entry point.

## Key Metrics

- **P/E Ratio:** 18.5x (vs industry avg 15x)
- **Free Cash Flow Yield:** 4.2%
- **Dividend Yield:** 2.5%
- **ROE:** 22%
- **Debt/Equity:** 0.3x

## Business Overview

QMC operates precision manufacturing facilities serving aerospace and defense end markets. Strong customer relationships, sticky revenue base, and consistent execution have created a quality compounder—but quality rarely comes cheap.

## Why We're Not Buying (Yet)

### Limited Margin of Safety

At 18.5x earnings, QMC trades at a premium to both the market and industry peers. While quality deserves a premium, we need a 30-40% margin of safety. Current valuation provides less than 10%.

### Cyclical Peak Concerns

Aerospace is at cycle highs. Defense budgets face potential pressure. While long-term dynamics remain favorable, near-term revenue could face headwinds that would compress multiples further.

### Better Opportunities Available

In a choice between "great business at fair price" vs "good business at great price," we're finding more compelling opportunities in the latter category currently.

## What Would Change Our Mind

We'd become buyers if:

1. **Price drops 20-25%** (pullback to $60-65 range)
2. **New contract wins** expand addressable market  
3. **Margin expansion** from automation initiatives
4. **Strategic acquisition** accretive to long-term value

## Watchlist Status

This is a "waiting for our pitch" situation. Quality businesses occasionally go on sale - patience is key.

**Monitor for:**
- Quarterly earnings (any guidance revisions)
- Aerospace industry data (potential demand softness)
- Valuation compression (multiple drops to 14-15x)

## The Bottom Line

QMC is exactly the type of business we want to own - just not at this price. We're patient. Good businesses tend to give us opportunities eventually.

**Rating:** HOLD (Watchlist)
**Price Target:** $85 (fair value)
**Would Buy At:** $60-65 (30%+ margin of safety)
