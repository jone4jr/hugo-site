### Advantages of MODAPTS

**Over Stopwatch Time Studies:**
- More consistent results
- Eliminates timing of abnormal performances
- Can develop standards before production starts
- Not affected by operator pace
- Faster data collection
- Better for method improvement

**Over MTM:**
- Faster to apply (3-5x faster)
- Easier to learn
- More practical for shopfloor use
- Better for line balancing
- Less detailed when detail isn't needed

**Over Work Sampling:**
- Provides element-level detail
- Enables method improvement
- More accurate for short cycles
- Better for balancing operations
