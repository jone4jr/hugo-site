5AM Shift - Daily Leadership Rhythm Card

Morning start
- What is the single most important outcome today?
- What risk could block it?
- Who owns the next move?
- What standard needs extra attention?

Midday check
- Are we on plan, ahead, or behind?
- What changed since the start of the day?
- What needs escalation before it becomes late?
- Who needs coaching or support now?

Closeout
- What was completed?
- What carried over?
- Who owns the carryover tomorrow?
- What did we learn that should change tomorrow's plan?

Use the same questions every day until the rhythm becomes normal.
